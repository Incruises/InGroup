Origins

INGROUP INTERNATIONAL LLC was formed in San Juan Puerto Rico in 2018 as our founders moved the international headquarters and themselves to the “Isla del Encanto” (the Island of Enchantment). 

The move boosted global expansion within the cruise industry while developing additional value in resorts and hotel vacation options. 

INGROUP is now a vibrant participant in the Puerto Rico economy and makes an ever-increasing impact. As our primary sub-brand, inCruises® expands its strong independent sales team (called Partners) INGROUP employs ever-increasing numbers of operations, accounting, human resources and technical personnel from the local workforce and looks towards a bright future. 

Brands

The current INGROUP brands reflect the company’s vision and strength as the first to market Membership model focused on the cruise industry, then expanding into hotels and resorts.

Charitable Causes
